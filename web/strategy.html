Strategy & Roadmap

How Autonomi protects your position, and where we're headed next.

How Autonomi Works

  1. 1

    Deposit & Borrow

    You deposit USYC (tokenized Treasury) as collateral and borrow USDC on Arc. Your position has a loan-to-value ratio (LTV). As USYC price or your borrows change, LTV moves.

  2. 2

    Agent Monitors 24/7

    Our backend agent runs on a schedule (e.g. every 5 minutes), reads USYC price and your position from the contract, and checks if LTV has crossed the safety threshold (e.g. 65%).

  3. 3

    Rebalance & Notify

    When LTV is too high, the agent calls autoRebalance(user, targetLTV) on-chain to bring your position back to target (e.g. 60%). If you've registered a phone number, you get an SMS. Webhooks fire for integrations.

Product Roadmap

Phase 1 — MVP (Live)

Autonomi contract on Arc Testnet: collateralised borrowing, LTV monitoring, agent-triggered rebalancing. Web dashboard with wallet connect, live positions, and USYC yield view. SMS alerts (one number per wallet, owner-verified). REST API v1: positions, agent status, alerts, API keys, webhooks (rebalance / warning / price), analytics and monitoring. OpenAPI docs and developer portal.

Shipped

Phase 2 — Governance & UX

DAO or owner governance for LTV caps and agent allowlist. Circle-managed wallets integration. Enhanced dashboard: agent activity log, historical rebalances, and richer notification preferences.

Target: 2025

Phase 3 — Scale & Production

Mainnet deployment. Stork oracle integration for production USYC price feeds. Multi-chain expansion via Arc. Institutional-grade analytics and risk dashboards.

Target: 2025+

Tech Stack

Chain & Oracles

  • Arc — Execution layer and testnet
  • Stork — Price oracle (USYC feeds, production)
  • Circle — USDC, USYC, managed wallets

Smart Contracts

  • Solidity — Autonomi, MockTeller, MockUSYC
  • Foundry — Build, test, deploy
  • OpenZeppelin (Ownable, ReentrancyGuard)

Backend & Web
